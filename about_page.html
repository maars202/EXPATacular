Who We Are

We are a group of friends that wanted to solve the problem of integration for potential expats coming to work in Singapore.

It is a common experience for expats to feel anxious before their trip to Singapore due to the unfamiliarity of the place. They would need to go on many platforms to gather the information on Singapore to prepare themselves.

This is where we come in. Our platform is a one-stop solution to all these woes. It is where everyone can get a holistic understanding of Singapore culure and essential information they need, while finding a like-minded community to share tips and experiences.
